What is Mihoyo or Hoyoverse?

Are you guys like playing video game or watch animation, maybe you guys know Genshin Impact, Honkai Impact 3rd and Honkai Star rail. This game has been popular for a year. However, do you know the company that made this game? and why they made this game?

THe company who make this game is Mihoyo. Mihoyo or what we know as Hoyoverse is an animation studio and video game company based in Shanghai, China. the independent game developer miHoYo was founded by 3 students from Jiao Tong University, Shanghai named Cai Haoyu, Liu Wei, and Luo Yuhao. The three of them are ACG (Anime, Comic, Game) lovers. The company is also famous for its slogan, "Tech Otaku's Save the World".

Mihoyo or hoyoverse release they first game in 2011, the game name in Fly me 2 The Moon, a side scrolling game with anime stitches. Mihoyo changed its name outside of China to what we know as Hoyoverse on February 15, 2022.
